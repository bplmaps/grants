Chambered Nautilus (Nautilus pompilius)

To make their way toward the Pacific whaling grounds, the Roman 2nd sailed from the Desolation Islands in the south toward New Holland and New Zealand. The chambered nautilus is distributed as far south as Tasmania, southeast of the continental island, where the ship would make its way north into warmer waters.
