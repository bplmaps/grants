Little Syria, or Syriantown, thrived from the late 1880s through the 1950s in what is today Chinatown and part of the South End of Boston. Few visible traces remain. The Boston Little Syria Project aims to document its history in collaboration with the neighborhood's former residents and their descendants. This map was developed with Ian Spangler and supported by the Leventhal Map and Education Center.
